## Objective
This course and associated credential (IOZ-110) will validate your knowledge of IoT terminology and your ability to understand the components of IoT infrastructure, help you uncover challenges for consideration and understand the impact that IoT has on your organization. This includes being able to identify what IoT can do for your organization and the various business and technical challenges that you must address.

## Summary
The Internet of Things (IoT) allows organization to collect an extraordinary volume of data and new analysis tools can enable you to use this data to make well-informed decisions to be made quickly. But before you can successfully plan and implement an IoT solution, you must understand the various factors that will drive your decisions. Putting IoT systems into place can be a complicated proposition with unique considerations distinctly different from traditional IT solutions. This course will familiarize you with IoT components, understand the challenges that you may face, and make design decisions about your IoT projects.
